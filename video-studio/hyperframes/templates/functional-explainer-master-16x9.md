# Functional Explainer Master Template - 16:9

## Role
Canonical master template for Summa Social functional explainers in 16:9.

## Purpose
Use this template to turn a production brief plus real captures into a durable explainer composition.
This is the approved path for future feature explainers.

## Canonical inputs
- Production brief
- Real product capture registry
- Reusable shot definitions
- Approved scene copy

## Output contract
- 16:9 explainer composition
- Four-scene structure: hook, problem, solution, outcome
- Product-led tone
- One main idea per scene
- One overlay at most per scene

## Non-negotiable rules
- Use real captures as the visual proof layer.
- Do not treat `05-devolucions-estat-real-16x9` as the master template.
- `05` may be studied as research, but it is not the canonical precedent.
- Do not promote research-only material into the master path.

## Template fields
- `title`
- `brief`
- `capture_set`
- `shot_map`
- `scene_order`
- `overlay_rules`
- `render_notes`
- `status`

## Notes for derived pieces
- Keep scene logic stable.
- Swap only the content that the brief and capture set justify.
- If a future piece needs new proof, add new captures instead of inventing visual evidence.

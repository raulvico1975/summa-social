# Product Film Template - 16:9

## Role

Canonical 16:9 editorial template for premium Summa product films.

## Purpose

Use this template only after a proof-first master has been approved.
It exists to turn capture-backed truth into a strong public-facing film.

## Required inputs

- approved proof-first brief
- approved capture registry
- proof-first master or equivalent verified sequence
- shot language from `product-film-shot-language.md`

## Output contract

- 16:9 product film
- 3 to 5 proof beats
- real UI as the primary visual layer
- minimal editorial copy
- website-grade visual quality

## Default composition law

- prefer full-bleed UI or near-full-bleed UI
- keep text outside the proof area unless it is part of the product
- use scale, crop, and timing to guide the eye
- avoid framed-card product layouts by default

## Non-negotiable rules

- no reuse of the rejected `05` pilot language
- no assumption that `06` is a premium precedent
- no fixed beat names beyond the shot language jobs
- no overlay system that becomes a template in itself

## Reuse note

Reuse the template only if the new piece still wants a sober editorial film.
If the story demands another format, write a new template instead of stretching
this one.

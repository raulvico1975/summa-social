# Detail Macro

## Purpose
Reveal material quality, interface precision, texture, or a small functional detail that supports the product story.

## When to use
Use after the establishing shot when the film needs proof of craft, design quality, or a feature close-up.

## Motion profile
Micro-dolly, rack focus, slight push, or slow reframing. Keep movement controlled so the detail remains easy to inspect.

## Copy allowance
0 to 3 words max. Prefer labels, callouts, or a single attribute instead of full sentences.

## Rejection triggers
- The detail is not legible at the target viewing size.
- The shot only looks good because of lighting but does not prove anything about the product.
- The frame requires multiple labels to make sense.
- The move hides the detail instead of revealing it.

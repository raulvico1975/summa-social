# Proof Outcome

## Purpose
Close the story with a credible result, success state, or finished condition that proves the value of the product.

## When to use
Use near the end of the film when the audience needs a final answer instead of another feature view.

## Motion profile
Slow settle, clean hold, or a minimal reveal into a stable end frame. The ending should feel decisive, not busy.

## Copy allowance
0 to 6 words max, usually a result statement, metric, or final claim with no extra explanation.

## Rejection triggers
- The outcome is not backed by a real product state or capture.
- The claim is broad enough to need footnotes in-frame.
- The ending feels like a generic ad close instead of a product proof point.
- The shot repeats earlier material without adding a result.

# Hand Interaction

## Purpose
Show the product being used by a person so the film demonstrates touch, scale, and human intent.

## When to use
Use when the brief needs usage clarity, tactile credibility, or a natural bridge between product beauty and action.

## Motion profile
Natural follow, short stabilization, and restrained camera correction. Keep the movement close to the interaction and avoid showy handheld energy.

## Copy allowance
0 to 5 words max, usually a verb phrase or a short functional cue.

## Rejection triggers
- The hand action is unclear or looks staged beyond credibility.
- The user movement blocks the product instead of clarifying it.
- The scene requires a lot of text to explain the action.
- The interaction cannot be supported by an actual capture or approved production rule.

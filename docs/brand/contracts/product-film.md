# Product Film Contract

## Purpose

`product-film` is the public-facing premium video contract for Summa Social.

It takes one approved proof-first master and turns it into an editorial product
film that can live on the website, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or sales surfaces
without becoming generic SaaS motion.

## Canonical chain

`source capture -> proof-first master -> product film -> delivery cuts`

This means:

- the proof-first master carries workflow truth
- the product film carries editorial quality
- delivery cuts inherit the same story and only adapt framing, duration, or
  export profile

## Visual law

- real Summa UI is the primary image
- product footage should feel full-bleed, not boxed inside a poster card
- typography is minimal, intentional, and secondary to the proof
- motion directs attention but never substitutes for proof
- the frame must feel calm, exact, and expensive

## What the film must not do

- no framed dashboard card as the default composition
- no big poster headline plus embedded app window as the core layout
- no fixed `hook -> problem -> solution -> outcome` template
- no decorative lower thirds or caption rails as a house style
- no gradients, glows, or overlays that try to create quality by decoration
- no copy density that turns the piece into a slide

## Story structure

Use `3` to `5` proof beats.

Each beat must have:

- one job
- one proof target
- one visible state change or confirmation

Valid beat jobs are:

- `establish`
- `orient`
- `isolate`
- `verify`
- `resolve`
- `hold`

## Copy rules

- one dominant line at most when a line is needed
- no supporting line unless it removes ambiguity
- if the UI already explains the moment, copy should disappear
- every word must serve the viewer, not the brand ego

## Rejection triggers

- it looks like a template rather than a film
- it could belong to any SaaS company
- it asks typography to do the work of proof
- it frames the product like a keynote slide instead of a living tool
- it feels busy, salesy, or ornamental
- it cannot be understood as a sequence of proof beats

## Governance

`product-film` is downstream of `functional-explainer`.

If the proof-first master is weak, the fix is upstream.
If the master is solid and the piece still feels poor, the fix belongs here.
